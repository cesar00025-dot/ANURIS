# Why ANURIS?

Publishing photos or videos without protecting them may expose your identity or allow your content to be redistributed without authorization.

**ANURIS** was designed to help content creators protect their material before publishing it by combining automatic face censorship, intelligent watermarking and professional editing tools in a single application.
